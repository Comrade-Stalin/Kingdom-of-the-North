The Winter Guard

Lord-Commander Cregan Stark

Son of King Rykard Stark and Lord of Castle Cerwyn. Raised in the crucible of the North and trained alongside its finest warriors. A tactician known for cool resolve and unshakable loyalty.

Lord Gared Glover

Master of War and Lord of Deepwood Motte. Nigh-undefeated duelist and battle-hardened commander. He led the vanguard in the River Rebellion with unmatched valor and blood-won glory.

Lord Galanor Flint

Lord of Flint’s Finger. Known for his stoic nature and formidable discipline. A quiet force in the field, unyielding and sharp-eyed, ever watchful from the North's rocky shores.

Lord Padrik Bolton

Master of Arms and Lord of the Dreadfort. A grim but loyal warrior who forged his reputation during the River Rebellion. His stern training regimens are feared and respected alike.

Lord Paul Mormont

Lord of Bear Island. A seasoned veteran of many skirmishes in the west and beyond the Wall. Fiercely honorable and brutally effective — his axe speaks where words fail.
