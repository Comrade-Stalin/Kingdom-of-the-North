The Winter Guard

The Winter Guard is the elite military order sworn to protect the King in the North and enforce the will of House Stark. They are composed of veteran knights, loyal bannermen, and seasoned warriors from across the realm.

Duties

  • Serve as the King’s personal guard and strike force
  • Escort high-ranking officials and safeguard Winterfell
  • Lead wartime campaigns under the command of the Lord-Commander

Command Structure

  • Lord-Commander: Ser Cregan Stark
  • Senior Captain: Selected by merit and trial
  • Watch Lieutenants: Tasked with patrolling key roads and borderlands

Traditions

Members of the Guard wear black cloaks with silver wolf brooches and take oaths of loyalty to the King before the Heart Tree in Winterfell’s godswood. Many see their service as a sacred calling.
